KRATOS

Voiced by Christopher Judge

Kratos is a Spartan Warrior, Atreus Father and Greek God of War. Kratos spent much of that time wandering the earth alone, battling his inner demons. He eventually settles in Scandinavia, where the Norse gods and myths roam. He meets a woman named Faye and settles down with her. They have a son, who Kratos names Atreus.

ATREUS

Voiced by Sunny Suljic

Atreus is son of Kratos and Faye and a Half-God and Half-Giant. He is incredibly adept at learning languages and using magic. His mother taught him all about the Norse gods and myths. She also taught Atreus to hunt and speak many languages.

FREYA

Voiced by Danielle Bisutti

Freya is Vanir Goddess of Love and former Queen of the Valkyries. She was previously in an abusive marriage to Odin, who mockingly gave her the nickname Frigg, and is the mother of Baldur and the twin sister of Freyr. Odin banished her to Midgard for her betrayal as she tried to leave him for his abuse and his countless acts of cruelty against the Jötnar.

BALDUR

Voiced by Jeremy Davies

Baldur is the son of Odin and Freya and Brother of Thor and Tyr. To protect her son from his death due to prophecy, Freya cast a spell of immortality on Baldur, which results in him not feeling anything. Odin sent him after Kratos and Atreus to kill Kratos' wife Faye in order to prevent Ragnarök, but was unaware that she was already dead and had been tracking her by her ashes that Kratos was carrying.

BROK

Voiced by Robert Craighead

Brok is the Brother of Sindri and a Dwarf from Swartalfaheimn. Brok is one of the Dwarven merchants and blacksimths in God of War. With the necessary materials, Brok can make or improve items for Kratos and Atreus. He and his brother, Sindri, had created Mjölnir and the Leviathan Axe, a powerful magic axe with ice magic.

SINDRI

Voiced by Adam J. Harrington

Sindri is the brother of Brok and one of the dwarven Merchants and Blacksmith in God of War. He is also the dwarf who forged the hammer Mjolnir of legend. You can sell found items to him for In-game currency named 'Hacksilver'. He will ugrade of forge you new armour, If you've got the coin and resources.

MIMIR

Voiced by Alastair Duncan

Mimir is a Norse god of Wisdom and Knowledge. He was Odin's advisor before until odin imprisoned him 109 years before. Odin tortures him everyday. Mimir can sacrifice anything to get out of the imprisonment. Mimir have vast knowledge of the Norse mythology, gods and world, but there are things that even he does not know.

MAGNI

Voiced by Troy Baker

Magni was the eldest son of Thor and Sif and eldest brother of Modi. Both Magni and his brother Modi and accompany their uncle, Baldur, on his mission to hunt down Kratos and Atreus, with the aim of bringing them to Odin in Asgard.

MODI

Voiced by Nolan North

Modi was the Norse God son of Thor and Sif, the younger brother of Magni and older brother of Thrúd.He also can shoot lightning, and uses a mace and shield. He ran off after father killed his brother, Magni.
